About Me

Hello! I'm a passionate computer science student with a love for tackling challenging problems, especially those that can be solved with artificial intelligence and machine learning. I am currently in my fourth year at San Diego State University, set to graduate in the spring of 2025.

From a young age, I've been fascinated by the intersection of technology and problem-solving. This curiosity led me to pursue a degree in computer science, where I've had the opportunity to explore various programming languages, algorithms, and technologies.

Throughout my academic journey, I've delved into the intricacies of AI and machine learning, finding joy in understanding how these technologies can be applied to real-world problems. Whether it's developing predictive models, optimizing algorithms, or exploring new data structures, I'm always eager to expand my knowledge and skills in this rapidly evolving field. My most recent project involved using computer vision to classify ASL finger spellings into their correct classes.

Beyond academics, I'm an avid learner and enjoy staying updated on the latest advancements in technology. I actively seek out opportunities to engage with the tech community, whether through attending workshops or participating in hackathons.

In addition to my technical skills, I also value collaboration and teamwork. I currently work part-time as a server at Din Tai Fung, which is world-renowned for its food and impeccable service. Working part-time while attending university has taught me how to manage my time effectively to stay on top of my schoolwork while upholding my commitment to my work schedule.

Outside of the classroom, you can often find me experimenting with smart home devices, cooking new recipes in the kitchen, or taking care of my numerous houseplants. I believe in maintaining a healthy work-life balance, as I believe it contributes to overall productivity and well-being.

I'm excited about the opportunity to leverage my skills and passion for technology to make a meaningful impact in the world. Whether it's through developing innovative solutions, contributing to groundbreaking research, or inspiring the next generation of technologists, I'm dedicated to making a positive difference in whatever I do.

Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit about me. I look forward to connecting and exploring potential opportunities together.
